Quick answers

Frequently asked questions by our community

How do I reset all my transactions?
Go to the main Tracker page → scroll to the bottom of transactions panel → click "Reset All Data". You'll be prompted to confirm before deletion.
Can I change the currency?
Yes! Click the globe icon on the top bar of the main app. Choose from 30+ currencies. Your preference is saved automatically.
How do I export my financial data?
You can export your financial data by navigating to the "Analytics Dashboard" menu and selecting "Export to Excel". It will then be generated for download.
How does collaboration work?
Click the users icon in the main app, click on "Create New Group" if you are the owner, click on "Join Existing Group" if you are joining someone and simply put the code. All transactions after joining a group will be shared in the household menu.
